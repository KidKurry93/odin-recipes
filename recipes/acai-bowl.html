Acai Bowl Recipe

Description

Ready in just a few minutes, this Acai Bowl Recipe is filled with acai berry puree, fruit, and all your favorite toppings. Learn how easy it is to make your very own Acai Bowl right from home and enjoy this powerhouse of fiber, antioxidants, and healthy fats anytime you want for a fraction of the cost!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup acai berry sorbet
  • 2 tbs granola, or as desired
  • 4 sliced strawberrries
  • 1 sliced banana
  • 2 tsp unsweetened coconut flakes, or as desired
  • 1 tsp honey, or as desired

Directions

  1. Place acai sorbet in a bowl
  2. Place a top layer of granola
  3. Line sliced strawberrries on top of granola layer on one side
  4. Line slcied bananas on top of toop of granola layer on other side
  5. Sprinkle top with coconut flakes
  6. Drizzle honey over toppings
  7. Enjoy!
